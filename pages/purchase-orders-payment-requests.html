Purchase Orders & Payment Requests

Purchase Orders (“POs”) are required for every cost incurred on a production, including AMEX charges and location checks.

Purchase Order Tutorial

Fill out all of the information on the PO completely on Zoho. Provide the following information:

• Job name and number

• Name of vendor & contact

• Vendors full address

• Price/Cost

• Line number

List an amount on a PO when seeking credit card authorization signatures from the EP. There is an estimate column on the POs that should be filled out with this information before seeking the EP’s signature.

• All POs must be signed by either the Producer or Head of Production.

• Multiple invoices for the same vendor may go on one PO (do not combine multiple vendors on one PO)

• Each invoice should be listed separately on the PO and PO Log

Indicate on your PO if Lord Danger is owed any money (for credits, returns, etc.) or if there was loss or damage. Please provide a detailed description of the reasons for money owed or any damage/loss.

Credit Card purchases must be made out to the vendor and not to the credit card, i.e. Amex. The last 4 digits of the card number must be indicated on the PO next to the checked box labeled "Credit Card".

Copies of POs

All PO’s must be digitized in the drive into its own folder following this format:

PO # - Job Number - Vendor

001-#300 Galpin

002-#300 J.L. Fisher

Each folder should include the PO, any vendor paperwork, agreements, COI’s, cc auths, invoices, and W9’s or receipts.
