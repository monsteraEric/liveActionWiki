COVID (Materials)

Lord Danger has a COVID compliance document included in each project directory: 04_shoot⁩ ▸ ⁨07_templates_resources⁩ ▸ ⁨_covid_materials⁩

Please be sure to read this document and ensure your production follows these protocols! You may also share it with your crew when sending out call sheets.

NOTE:

Beginning in August of 2021, all on-set personnel must verify they are fully vaccinated at least 2 weeks prior to being on set. All must provide, either digitally or physically, their vaccination card to the Covid Compliance Officer before entering the set.

Per the CDC, “fully vaccinated” means receiving both (2) doses of the Pfizer and Moderna vaccines or (1) single dose of the Johnson and Johnson vaccine.

Per the CDC, it takes 2 weeks for a fully vaccinated person to build immunity to COVID-19.

More information can be found here: https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/vaccines/different-vaccines/how-they-work.html
