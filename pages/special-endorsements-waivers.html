Special Endorsements and Waivers of Subrogation

If a vendor requests a Certificate of Insurance with any special endorsements and/or a Waiver of Subrogation, inform the Executive Producer immediately. Please note that additional costs may be incurred. With that said, it is standard practice to provide a Waiver of Subrogation when filming at Universal Studios. Please allow one day to process paperwork.
