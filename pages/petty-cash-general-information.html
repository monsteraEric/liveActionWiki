General Information (Petty Cash)

Petty Cash

The Production Manager and/or Assistant PM/Operations Manager are responsible for each production‘s adherence to these petty cash procedures and for submitting the petty cash log upon completion of the project.

Obtaining Petty Cash

One purchase order made out to Petty Cash is to be used for each production. Petty Cash requests for a maximum of $5,000. Please note we will request a breakdown of PC allocation if more than 2 requests are made on any one project.

The Petty Cash requests must include the Production Manager’s name with the amount being requested clearly stated on the PO and the denominations of the currency. PC will be delivered the next day to the Production Manager.

These requests should be emailed to our accountant listed at the beginning of this manual.

Alternatively, if we need to send a PA to the bank, please make the check out to Petty Cash with the person’s name picking it up. This will be the only person allowed by the bank to pick up cash. If there is a change, notify the AP immediately. Accounting must also obtain a copy of the driver’s license of the person who will be going to the bank to pick up the cash.

Dispensing Petty Cash

One person (the Production Manager or Operations Manager) is responsible for dispensing petty cash to the crew. This person will be held accountable for all petty cash until the conclusion of the job. All cash outlays to crew members must be signed for individually, and accounted for by that employee. Before handing off any petty cash the AP will give you a petty cash agreement form that must be filled out along with extra copies for the production manager to disperse as cash is dispersed. All cash must be logged and allocated correctly. AP will provide you with a petty cash expense form to fill out, if the producer has their own sheets that work as well this is fine, however, please confirm with AP.

Expenses Not Allowed in Petty Cash

The following items will not be reimbursed through petty cash. Please make all crew members receiving petty cash aware of these rules prior to disbursing:

Any and all parking tickets. The company will not reimburse tickets for personal or rented vehicles.

Restaurant stubs / No restaurant chits.

All restaurant receipts must state the first and last names of the persons dining and the business purpose for the meal. This rule must be strictly adhered to or petty cash cannot be reimbursed.

Meal receipts over $15

If lunches total is more than $15, only $15 will be reimbursed, no exceptions.

Gasoline charges for use of personal car/transportation.

Gas Receipts will only ever be accepted for use with applicable Production Vehicles.

For gas receipts, write the license plate number of the production vehicle.

Gas Receipts will not be accepted for personal vehicles. See mileage on timecard for vehicle reimbursement.

Do not net out receipts.

Actualize each charge and each credit.

Liquor, cigarettes, marijuana, or other substances for crew members, director, agency, client, etc.

Labor & Wages

All labor must be paid through payroll.

Personal items, these items include toiletries, aspirin, etc.

Handwritten receipts.

Submit legitimate vendor receipts that indicate the nature, date & amount of the expense and bear the vendor‘s name. Adding machine tape will not be accepted.

Telephone/Cell phone Bills.

Photocopied receipts.

Only original receipts are acceptable. Do not submit copies of receipts.

Credit Card receipts.

All credit card charges must be reimbursed by a PO.

Do not reimburse credit card charges with petty cash.

Ensure that transactions were not paid by debit card or credit card, these will not be accepted.

Car Service.

Any taxi or car service must have the following explanation on the receipt: Name of Passenger, Destination, and Purpose for Car Service.

Vendor Credits

All credits coming in the form of a gift card or company check must be submitted on a purchase order.

Vendors shall not be paid in the form of cash, and may not be submitted on the PC envelope.

Cash Received for Return of Merchandise Purchased with Petty Cash

If a purchase is made from petty cash and the company issues cash for the return, submit the original receipt along with the original return receipt in your petty cash envelope and enter both the receipt and the return amount into the corresponding line on the envelope.

Amex Purchases

If possible, please use the business AMEX as it is much easier to track than cash. Please see Operations Manager for a business AMEX.
