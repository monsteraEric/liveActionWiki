Petty Cash Envelopes

Lord Danger petty cash envelopes must be used for petty cash reconciliations. Please see the Operations Manager to obtain envelopes. No other type of envelope will be accepted.

Petty cash may not be advanced to another job, even if the same person produces both jobs.

All envelopes must be approved and processed by department heads prior to being submitted.
