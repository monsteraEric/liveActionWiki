Credit Cards

Lord Danger Corporate American Express

The Executive Producer’s corporate AMEX is to be used for most purchases i.e. airline tickets, hotels, car service, vehicle rentals, Lord Danger preferred vendors, vendors who do not provide payment terms, etc. All POs are to be submitted to OLC as charges are approved by the EP. It may also be used for production and is actually preferred to cash as cash gets lost and is untraceable.

Please see “Purchase Orders” for proper procedures on paperwork.

Setting up a Credit Card Authorization Form: Credit card authorization forms will not be approved without a PO – please present both forms to the EP to obtain approval of Amex use.

All travel costs are to go on the EP’s Lord Danger AMEX. All points are to go to Lord Danger, no exceptions.

There may be cases where vendors will need a CC authorization form filled out to run the order. Please use this card pending approval from the Head of Production to charge the card.

Personal Credit Cards

Please do not use personal credit cards. If a personal credit card must be used, get approval from accounting first. For reimbursement, a PO must be filled out made out to the cardholder. List the name of the cardholder, attach the original invoice, receipt, and credit card statement corresponding to the charges. Photocopies of the receipt will not be accepted. Reimbursement checks will be processed within 3-5 days after receipt of paperwork. Airline and hotel charges are not to be charged to personal credit cards.
