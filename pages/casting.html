Casting Documentation Procedures

The Casting Director should inform production immediately of any needs for a Taft Hartley before booking non-SAG talent on a SAG project. Production must inform the Agency immediately. Agency Producer must also be notified via email when and talent incurs penalties.

(i.e. third callback, wardrobe session OT, meal penalties, and OT on set.)

Copies and scans of all signed talent contracts with 2 pictures (head & shoulders and full body) should be maintained for the wrap and originals sent to the agency.

Station 12s are to be completed with copies in the talent wrap section for all principal talent (if it is a SAG shoot).

If children or babies are being cast, please refer to the SAG & Labor law rules in the talent section of this manual.
