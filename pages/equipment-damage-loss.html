Equipment Damages & Loss

Immediately notify the Executive Producer of any potential claims. Complete the First Notice of Claim Form. The form will require the following information:

The name and address of the studio/production house/location where the incident occurred

Rental Agreement

Contact at the incident location for further information.

Date of claim.

A police report if available. If a copy of the report is not available, provide the name of anyone who investigated the incident.

How the incident happened.

Persons/property involved.

The estimated amount of damage or loss for each vendor.

Was a reshoot necessary and, if so, the estimated cost to do so.

Obtain photographs of the scene of the incident and any resulting damage.

Any additional information which may be helpful.

Copy of certificate of insurance if available.
