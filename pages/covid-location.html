Location (Covid)

Location

The location needs to be prepped and marked to enforce social distancing and all safety protocols before the shoot / general call.

Select separate rooms, areas, tents, etc for all crew & personnel.

Tape or mark off 6’ apart line makers for crafty, catering, any other place people congregate.

All seating and holding areas are to be comprised of individual chairs 6’ apart. This includes meals.

Locations need to be cleaned and disinfected before and after our shoot.

Regular disinfecting must continue throughout the day.

All surfaces, tables, shared equipment (cameras, grip equipment, etc) to be cleaned regularly throughout the day.

Posted Reminders

COVID safety signage and rules must be posted repeatedly in crew-populated spaces throughout the location. Posted warnings should include the following reminders:

Stay at least 6’ apart

Wear face-coverings at all times

Wash & disinfect hands frequently
