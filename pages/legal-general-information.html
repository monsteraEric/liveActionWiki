General Information (Legal)

General Notices

Do not sign any legal documents (including location and equipment agreements), send them directly to the Senior Producer.

Any allegations of libel, slander, or invasion of privacy against Lord Danger or a crewmember must be reported to the Managing Director or Executive Producer immediately by phone. If legal documents are involved, forward them directly to the Managing Director and EP and allow them time to reply directly.

Compliance

Production must comply with all federal, state, county, and city laws and regulations pertaining to filming, labor practices, and fire and safety codes regardless of where filming occurs.
