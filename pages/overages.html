Overages

An overage form is required for all overages. Be sure to include mark-up and insurance on overages, where they apply (check with Executive Producer). The overage form must be signed by the Agency Producer and Line Producer before additional monies can be spent.

If the Agency Producer agrees to the overage but refuses to sign, do not go forward with the expenditures until the Executive Producer gives the authorization to do so. At wrap, include a copy of all pending and approved overages in the wrap book, and include a copy of the overages in folder number

Note that all overages must be signed in order to actualize them in the Producer‘s Actual. Also, attach copies of all overages with the Producer‘s actual when emailing the Producer‘s actual to the Managing Director and Executive Producer.

*Please outline all signed overages within the wrap notes.

An overage form template is found on the Freelance Wiredrive, within the “Forms” folder.
