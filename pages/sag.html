Lord Danger is not SAG signatories therefore neither company may act as such or pay SAG talent without becoming a Signatory or using the One-Production-Only Agreement.

Please remember to complete or obtain from the 2nd AD an Exhibit G Time Report for all fittings and shoot days. Submit the original copy to the Agency with the contracts and keep a copy for the Wrap Binder, as well as upload to Google Drive.

Below is some basic information regarding SAG talent. This by no means represents all applicable rules and regulations. Please address all questions to the Executive Producer or Agency Producer. Please note that hourly and daily rates have not been included due to ongoing contract negotiations.

Persons Covered

1. Principals: On-Camera (Speaking, the face is identifiable, in the foreground, demonstrating production or reaching to/off camera message, stunt/pilot/driver, group, dancers, singers, speakers)

2. Principals: Off-Camera (narrators, announcers, puppeteers, and singers)

3. Hand Models

4. General Extras: 1st 40 hired (stand-ins and photo doubles are not part of a 40 count)

From the American Association of Advertising Agencies:

“Extras are background performers, as long as he/she appears in the background and does not speak lines or sing. An extra CAN be directed; an extra CAN hold a product, consume the product, or demonstrate it; an extra CAN be recognizable-as long as the performer remains in the background. Extras can also appear in the foreground. To do this and NOT be paid as a Principal Performer, they must be used only as atmosphere. Extras must be paid for each commercial worked in a single day. Extras can double in one or more roles in a single commercial without receiving additional pay.”
