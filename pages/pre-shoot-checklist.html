Pre-Shoot Checklist

Before the first day of shooting, the following steps must be completed:

Discussion including director, editorial company, VFX supervisor, and post team to determine:

shooting frame rate,

base frame rate,

Codec for shooting

Format for shooting

Proxies or transcodes required/desired

LUT information

Where hard drives are going and to whom after the shoot

Confirm that we have someone to shoot BTS photos.
