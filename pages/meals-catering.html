Meals & Catering

All meals must be individually packaged.

All crafty must be individually packaged items.

No shared food items or silverware of any kind, including condiments, plastic silverware, plates, etc.

All food or crafty servers must wear masks & gloves, and sanitize on a regular basis.

All surfaces, tables, etc. near food serving areas must be sanitized on a regular basis.

Any waiting areas for food or craft service must enforce social distancing & mask wear.

Any menus etc. must be in a touchless format.

Drinks should be individually packaged. If they are from a communal container, they can only be served by the designated, protected meal or crafty server.
